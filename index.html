Catomino

A drawing game for on-the-go.
Cats are liquid! Stack your funny and weird cats. The 'catomino' is based on a classic video game 'Tetris' but now on the Paper. With the drawing template you can draw them endless. At the end your drawing can be used as a postcard with funny greetings.

Curriculum Vita

Sayoon Yang is a Nomad Designer from South Korea currently based in Halle, Germany. Her focus on Design is playfulness and multiple endings.

To understand the everyday's landscape, she studied Economics and finance and Media and Cultural studies at Hanyang University(Seoul, South Korea) from 2006 to 2011. After working in the field of social innovation and education business as a consultant - a lecturer and a project manager for several years, she started designing for her friends, who looked for someone who understands their aim and context.

It was real joy of her to visualize for the clients and for her projects as well. So she started studying Design of playing at learning at Burg Giebichenstein University of Art and Design Halle from 2017 to 2021. It drived her to realize her interest in handcraft and more interactive factors in design.
